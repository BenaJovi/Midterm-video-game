For this midterm assignment I was tasked to create a video game. The video game is to utilize a Z direction parallax that has objects moving towards the player. The player is to be a 3D model that is moved with arrow keys and have the camera follow him with every move. There are coins for him to collect which will trigger the level shift, every 5 coins collected, the level will change. If the player is hit 5 times by an object the level will restart but the score will be maintained.  

The game is about a monkey who is running from banana soldiers. The monkey has a dream to make it to the city of Los Angeles. He will travel form the jungle to a zoo and then enter the city. Every five coins he collects gets him closer to his dream. No money is free in this world, some coins are a lot harder to grab than it might look. Enjoy playing. 

General Rules:
left and right arrow keys move chimp
when a coin is collected a sound queue will happen.
when a soldier hits chimp he will turn red and yell.
when a level is reset the soldiers will be pushed back and chimp will turn green indicating that a level reset occurred. 
Each level has its unique music.
The dream is complete at 15 coins but if you collect 20 the game will automatically close out. 


The way i approached this assignment was utilizing what was taught in class and other assignments. Each lesson we did was a building block for a game and i just grabbed what i needed from those lessons. The overall world of the game was standard use of parallax images but creating moving 3D objects was challenging.
